Introduction

In this fun and engaging memory game, you'll have the opportunity to learn new Hungarian words, some very commonly used words from everyday life, like numbers. And the challenge? Match pairs of cards with the same word. You can test your memory and improve your vocabulary skills.

How to play?

  1. Click on a card, and reveal the hidden picture and word.
  2. Try to find matching pairs by remembering their positions.
  3. The game features 20 cards, so prepare for a good memory workout!

Remember, practice makes perfect! The more you play, the better your vocabulary will be. So dive in, and enjoy the game!
